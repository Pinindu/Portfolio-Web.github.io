About

Design that starts with
people, not pixels.

I'm Pinindu Chethiya, a UX designer from Sri Lanka with 5+ years of experience crafting human-centered digital products. I've worked with companies across Sri Lanka, Italy, and the United States — from telecoms serving millions to early-stage health-tech startups.

My approach is research-first: I study users deeply before touching a single screen. The result is design that feels intuitive — because it was built around real human behavior.
